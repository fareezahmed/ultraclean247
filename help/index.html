Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can cancel or reschedule your order up to 2 hours before your scheduled pickup time. Contact us via phone or email, and we'll be happy to help.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive SMS updates throughout the process. You can also call our customer service team for real-time updates on your order status.

What if I'm not home for pickup/delivery?

We offer contactless service. You can leave your laundry in a secure location, and we'll pick it up. For delivery, we can leave it in a safe spot or arrange a convenient time.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! Our express service offers same-day pickup and delivery for urgent needs. Additional charges apply. Contact us to check availability in your area.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets. NDIS funding is also available for eligible customers. Corporate clients can be invoiced.

How do I know my clothes are safe?

We use professional-grade equipment and follow strict quality control procedures. All garments are individually tagged and tracked throughout the process.
